Privacy is not just an option, but default, right at the core.

We are in no way tracking you or profiling you. The extension is completely private. The only time the extension get access to the "URL" you visit is when you open the extension by clicking it or by using the shortcut key ` Alt+Q `. Once the extension closes our permission is revoked. So be it Naughty or Nice, your website visits are always private from us.

So no need to worry about your browser actions. You can opt to use a VPN if you like to protect yourself from your ISP snooping.

The authifyURL database, the data source for this extension is hosted openly on Github for anyone to verify.
